IRIS FOUNDATION

IRIS FOUNDATION IS A NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANISATION FORMED STUDENTS TO PUT FORWARD CULTURAL,PHYSICAL AND EDUCATIONAL WELLNESS ALONG WITH PERSONALITY DEVOLOPEMENT.WE THE VOLUNTEERS OF IRIS FOUNDATION WORKS FOR THE REFORMATION OF OUR ILLUMINATIVE SOCIETY.WE HIGHLIGHTS THE POOR,BACKWARD COMMUNITY TO SATISFY THEIR NEEDS AND TO GAIN THEIR RIGHTS.

OUR WINGS

Blood Donors Wing

one of the core wings of IRIS FOUNDATION which mainly focuses to eradicate blood scarcity during medical emergencies through collective networking among the coordinators. Blood donation camps were conducted at various hospitals to address the shortage of blood in the blood bank

Medical Wing

Medical wing deals with the assessment and maintainance of overall Health condition of our society for a Healthy Generation Ahead.IRIS medical wing Aims to assess the health of population for maintaining healthy community by providing continuous Medical Checkups and financial support for the community who deserve it.

Social Wing

The Social Wing is a wing functioning under IRIS Foundation with a motive to spread social awareness , and empower human mind. Making critical analysis of social and political scenario under the surveillance of a group of active youngsters.

Phsycology Wing

The wing which helps people in their every day life . mission of this wing is to provide Mental support and free counselling , psychological education and awareness.

Media Wing

Media wing is a bunch of Creativity, which ameliorate the technical skills of members through various exercise. Moreover it's an irreplaceable factor by playing an important role in bringing the activities of IRIS FOUNDATION to the mainstream of the society.

Education Wing

Educational wing is a wing of wisdom, operating under the Iris Foundation. Finding educationally backward children and paving the way for them to progress,giving career guidance classes. As well as to help them prepare for competitive examinations by providing free classes

Environmental Wing

The Environmental Wing is a nature conservation wing operating under the IRIS. Its purpose is to do things related to nature and to work for studies and other things related to nature conservation.

Motivational Wing

Motivational wing runs under IRIS FOUNDATION . It's purpose is related to motivational thoughts , providing career guidance and Making awared to overcome current social scenerio of our society.

Creativity Wing

One of the eleven wings of IRIS Foundation which nurture the creative abilities and thinking of members as well as others. Also encouraging the creative talents through various programmes and Confluence.

Charity Wing

One of the core wings of IRIS FOUNDATION. The mission of which is to provide financial assistance to those who are financially backward in emergencies. Also engage in charitable activities as well as provide food and medicine to those in need.
